About Us

Pitas Nursery opened in 2017 located in Kg Taka, Pitas by starting the business by bringing in 5,000 oil palm seeds. Due to increasing demand in 2019, Pitas Nursery has moved to Kg Kalipuon, Pitas. In 2023, Pitas Nursery was upgraded to Pitas Nursery Sdn Bhd and successfully sold more than 24,000 oil palm seeds and seedlings per year.
